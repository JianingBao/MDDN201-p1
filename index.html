Parking system

Parking system is one of the most commen thing for those people who live in Wellington.They helped goverment to mange the morden world, all the parking space are connected with terminal by interenet, so when people pay for their parking, the system can connect them with bank and help manager to identify who paid for their parking space.

CCTV

CCTV also known as close-circuit television, this technology always used as a security devices. You can find many cameras in museum, bank, school or other place that need to be proceted. They will send their videos to cloud automatically though internet, and when the police or other people want to watch those videos, they can go to the cloud and download the video by connected to internet.

Powerlines are the most important thing in modern life, because they can transfer many things into different homes.
